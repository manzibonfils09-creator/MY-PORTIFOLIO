# 📁 Business

Strategic frameworks, venture thinking, and operational playbooks — built from the trenches of running a real company in Africa.

- **Pitch Frameworks** — Narrative structures for investor and client conversations
- **Operational Playbooks** — SOPs, client onboarding, logistics coordination

Everything here is battle-tested from building **Transafari Agency Ltd**, a premium ground logistics and luxury tourism company serving high-net-worth individuals across Rwanda.
